## From Execution to Mastery

Over 7.5 years, freelancing became more than work. It became a way of thinking. I learned to stay calm under pressure, adapt in uncertain conditions, and keep moving even when growth felt unstructured.

## The Builder Behind the Work

What makes my journey different is not only what I have done, but how I approach things:

- Supportive and strong at the same time
- Direct, yet genuinely caring
- Soft in emotion, but firm in decisions

I naturally take responsibility for my work and for people. I hold high standards because I believe growth requires honesty and effort.

## Beyond Work

Beyond work, I am driven by human impact. I especially care about supporting girls and students who are trying to build a future in difficult environments.

Through 1:1 guidance, I help with direction, confidence, and practical decisions in education and career growth.

I also enjoy:

- Cycling and hiking
- Traveling and learning through cultures
- Language learning (currently German B1/B2)

I carry a deep love for animals. I had a cat named Honey, who was part of my life for two years. Losing her this year reminded me how meaningful small connections can be.
